About Me

Hi, I’m Fatin Ayuni Hassan Sanusi! I’m a native of Pahang. Welcome to my digital space! I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology, Informatic Media with Honours from University Sultan Zainal Abidin, Terengganu.
